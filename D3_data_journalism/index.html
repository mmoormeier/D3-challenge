Health Risks by State

Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

There are many health risks that can be correlated to poverty, age and household income. Our data is organized by state which is represented by the blue dots in the chart. The Y axis has clickable links to see data by the percentage of people in that state that are obese, smokes or lacks healthcare. You make click on the headings on the Y axis to change the data. The X axis has clickable links to see data by the percentage of people in poverty, the median age or the median household income. Again, you may click on the links to change the data. By hovering your mouse over a state bubble, the data behind each value is displayed.
